Privacy Policy for Zubo Counter
Effective Date: 23 Jan. 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zubo Counter ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Data Collection and Usage

Zubo Counter is a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your device. All data you create within the App, including counters, their values, and their history, is stored exclusively on your device's local storage.

This data is never sent to our servers or any third-party services. We have no access to it.

2. Data Storage

All information and data generated by you while using Zubo Counter is stored securely in a local SQLite database on your mobile device. This data remains under your control at all times. If you uninstall the App, this data will be permanently deleted from your device.

3. Permissions

The App may request certain permissions (such as the INTERNET permission) which are standard for applications built with the Capacitor framework. However, we do not use these permissions to collect or transmit any of your personal data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
